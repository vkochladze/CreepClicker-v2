How To Play

Click the creeps to deal damage. When a creep's HP reaches 0, it grants gold based on the current creep's bounty.

Use the gold gained to:

  • Upgrade creeps. Upgraded creeps have a higher bounty, and a higher number of hitpoints.
  • Buy weapons: Buying weapons increases the damage dealt per click.
  • Buy items: Buying items grants automatic damage per second (DPS).
